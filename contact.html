FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services and processes.

What regions do you serve?
We provide logistics services across over 50 countries worldwide, with strong coverage across Asia, Europe, North America, and South America. Our partnerships enable us to offer comprehensive solutions regardless of your location.
How can I track my shipment?
You can track your shipments in real-time through our mobile app, available on both iOS and Android. Simply log in with your credentials and access the tracking dashboard for instant updates on your cargo location and status.
What types of cargo do you handle?
We handle a wide range of cargo including general merchandise, hazardous materials (with proper certifications), temperature-sensitive goods, oversized cargo, and high-value items. Contact us for specific requirements.
How do I start a partnership?
To explore partnership opportunities, please fill out the contact form selecting "Partnership" as your subject. Our business development team will review your inquiry and schedule a call to discuss potential collaboration.
What are your warehouse locations?
We operate over 200 distribution centers globally, with major hubs in China (Zhengzhou, Shanghai, Shenzhen), Europe (Germany, Netherlands), and North America (Los Angeles, New York). Contact us for specific warehouse availability.
Do you offer custom logistics solutions?
Yes, we specialize in tailored logistics solutions. Our team works closely with clients to design custom supply chain strategies that meet specific business needs, budget requirements, and timeline constraints.
